    More on the datatable keys

    The names in the datatable is a bit wierd and not always understandable, with time I'll make it more pretty. Right now it's just more or less a raw dump.

    Attackspeed of a meleeweapon in the game is calculated by:

    attackspeed = data['WeaponFetchingTime'] + data['FiringTime']
    Where WeaponFetchingTime is the time it takes for a weapon to begin the attack and FiringTime is the time it takes to execute the attack itself.
    DoHurtAnimTime, pretty sure it's how long a character will be "stunned" by a blow.
    Attackspeed and damage is used to calculate the overall DPS a weapon can output with:
    data["DpsCut"] = (data["CutDamageFactor"] * 100.0) / attackspeed
    data["DpsPuncture"] = (data["PunctureDamageFactor"] * 100.0) / attackspeed
