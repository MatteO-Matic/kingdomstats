Updated to 0.1.77.

Posted 14 Mar 2017

Thanks for the email notice about the update, I have now updated it to 0.1.77.
You might notice that the datatables didn't have any major changes with the new patch. They added all the T9 and T10 items to the game that was already in the files. I'm currently working on changelogs, it will be a preview over what items have changed achieved through comparing the new patch-data with the previous patch-data.

Official patch notes

Update Announcement (0.1.77)

9 March - Frittata
Dear gamers,
In order to update Tiger Knight to version 0.1.77, we will be bringing our servers down for maintenance on 10th March 2017.
Servers will be unavailable for approximately 3 hours, with the exact times as follows:
Asia server：2017/3/10 15:00-18:00（GMT+08:00）
NA server：2017/3/09 23:00 - 2017/3/10 02:00（PST）
EU server：2017/3/10 07:00-10:00（GMT）
If it fails to update normally please close Steam and relog in.

Update Content
  • New Advanced Battlefield units (tiers 9 & 10) will be available from the Upgrade screen.
    • Wei Leopard Knights
    • Wei Royal Elite Knights
    • Wei Central Guards
    • Wei Royal Cavalry Archers
    • Shu Royal Vanguard
    • Shu Royal Strike Troop
    • White Feather Warriors
    • Shu Royal Crossbow Cavalry
    • Hengjiang Shooters
    • Royal Archer
    • Wu Royal Elite Infantry
    • Jiefan Infantry
    • Late Empire Legionarius
    • Evocati Augusti
  • New game mode for Time-limited Battlefield: Command Mode - 7V7 Siege is open. 7V7 Conquest Mode will be temporarily closed.
    • Players can choose Time limited Battlefield during a set time each day,
    • Opening Time: 18:00-20:00 (same server time-zones as those used for maintenance)

Bugs Fixed
  • The Fire Arrow skill of the Jingzhou Crossbowman and the Shu Light Crossbowmen could cause horses to stagger incorrectly.
  • Roman adjutant behaves incorrectly after using his shield.
