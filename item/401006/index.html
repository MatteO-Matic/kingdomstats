Hua Xiong

Military general, good at leading close combat cavalry

Hua Xiong (also known as Ye Xiong) was under Dong Zhuo's command at the end of the Eastern Han as a military account governor. In the second year of the Chuping era, the northeastern warlords united against Dong Zhuo. Lu Bu, Hu Zhen, and Hua Xiong occupied and seized Yangrencheng, but Hu Zhen and Lu Bu didn't work well together, which caused difficulty during the siege. In addition, Lu Bu spread rumors that the enemy had attacked, allowing Sun Jian to seize the opportunity to win back the town. In the confusion, Hua Xiong was killed by Sun Jian's tracking army.
