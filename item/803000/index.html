Bo Cai

Balanced leader, can lead all sorts of troops

Rebel leader of the Yellow Scarves. In the first year of the Zhongping era (184 AD), he led the uprising in Yingchuan, defeating the Han right royal security general Zhu Jun. He took this opportunity to besiege Huangfu Song in Changshe, alarming officials across the land. However, Bo Cai set up camp in the adjacent grasslands, and when huge winds descended on him, he found himself trapped in Huang Pusong's ring of fire, forcing him to retreat to Yangdi.
