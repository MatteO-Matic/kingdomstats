Xianbei Riders



Xianbei, an ancient nationality which lived in the North of China. The Xianbei nationality originated from the Donghu, Xisu and Wuhuan people and were under the Xiongnu for a long time. After the East Han defeated the Northern Xiongnu, all Xianbei fled from the old domains of the Northern Xiongnu. Having absorbed part of the Xiongnu culture, the Xianbei rose up. Similar to the Wuhuan, the Xianbei people lived in settlements and became skillful in horsemanship and horseback archery; one could say these skills ran in their blood.
