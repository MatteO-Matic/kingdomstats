Zhongyuan Militia

Militias have existed since ancient times and were active in military operations for all states and countries during the warring period of the Han Empire.
The Zhongyuan Militia was made up mostly of farmers and city dwellers. Their equipment was issued by the cities, resulting in most of their weapons being extremely outdated spears. They often participated in defending the transportation of goods and the protection of cities.
They lacked training and had low morale, and were forced to join the military due to difficulties in their lives.
Due to the long struggle between the Northern Warlords, the Zhongyuan Militia were often without a fixed allegiance. After Cao Cao took over the north, they gradually became more stable reserve forces for war.
