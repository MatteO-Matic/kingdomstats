Sun Shangxiang

Commanding general, good at leading close combat infantry



Sun Shi (also known as Sun Shangxiang) was from Fuchun, Wu, and was known as "Sister of a General Who Attacks Barbarians of the Eastern Han, Sun Quan". She became Liu Bei's wife and was clever with a strong character. She often led around 100 female servants carrying knives. Even Liu Bei was worried about starting trouble with her. In the seventeenth year of Jian'an, Liu Bei occupied Shu and the relations of the Sun and Liu families worsened. Sun Quan sent soldiers to take back Madame Sun. Sun originally wanted to take Liu Chan away, but she was recaptured by Zhao Yun's army. Sun did not show up on any records after that.
