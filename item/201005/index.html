Ma Su

Intelligent leader, can lead close combat infantry and close combat cavalry

Ma Su, also known as Youchang, was a Shu Han official and general from Yicheng, Xiangyang, and the brother of Ma Liang. Ma Su was talented even when he was young, and was called one of "The Five Mas" along with his brothers. After serving in Jingzhou, he went with Liu Bei into Shu. Zhuge Liang saw his talent and thought highly of him. Near his end, Liu Bei once warned Zhuge Liang to be wary of Ma Su, but Zhuge Liang trusted him and discussed military affairs with him. In the sixth year of Jianxing, Ma Su was the pioneer of Zhuge Liang's northern expedition. Ma Su didn't listen to Wang Ping's persuasions and his team was assigned to Nanshan. In the end, defeat occurred when Zhang He cut off their water supply. Ma Su was later killed by Zhuge Liang and died a tearful death.
