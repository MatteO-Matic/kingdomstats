Wei Heavy Halberdiers



After the establishment of Cao Wei, warlord forces gradually moved towards the formal establishment of a central military.
Cao Wei's central troops were roughly equal to the Northern and Southern armies of the Han Dynasty and they took on the task of protecting the Su Wei palace and the capital.
Heavy halberd soldiers used polearms and dressed in full armor, and their main sources of troops came from the Bingzhou and Yanzhou heavy infantries. They relied on heavy hammers to fight cavalry and infantry and could also fiercely assault enemy infantry defenses. This force was active in Cao Wei's battle against Shu Han.
