Righteous White Horses



Righteous White Horses are Eastern Liaoning warlord, Gongsun Zan's entourage and they are comprised of 10 of the finest archers. In battle, they ride white horses and protect both of Gongsun Zan's flanks, earning the name "Righteous White Horses". After, this unit gradually expands, joining the thousands of guard cavalry. During the Eastern Liaoning frontier battles, the Righteous White Horses showed their intrepid battle efficiency and struck fear in the Wuhuan, Xianbei and other nomadic groups.
