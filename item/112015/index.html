Qingzhou Glaive Infantry

In the winter of 192 AD (the third year of the Chuping era), Qingzhou's Yellow Scarves were defeated by the coalition of Cao Cao and Bao Xin. Cao Cao received 300,000 soldiers and selected the best ones to join the Qingzhou army.
Qingzhou soldiers had strong bodies and were fully equipped, being one of the main forces of Cao Cao. Their special arrangement existed for twenty-eight years.
Qingzhou Glaive Infantry were the armored forces of the Qingzhou army. They dressed in heavy armor and were armed with long knives used to destroy enemy forces.
In Cao Cao and Lu Bu's battle for Yanzhou, the Qingzhou army's performance was a bad example of being quickly defeated by cavalry forces.
