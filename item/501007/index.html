Cohort Signifer

Commanding general, good at leading close combat infantry

A flag bearer representing the soul of the Ancient Rome's armies, bearing this banner was a position of great status. Frequently, flag bearers were veterans rich in combat experience. When at war, the Batallion's flag bearer would stand in the front line of the Batallion's formation. The flag bearer wore a bear hide and held a banner to symbolize glory and unity.
