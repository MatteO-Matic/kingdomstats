Jingzhou Sailors

The Jingzhou navy was strong and dominated the Yangtze River. During the reign of Liu Biao, the Jingzhou Navy was well known, as Huang Zu, Cai Mao, and other senior generals had a strong boat division. Jingzhou Sailors were equipped with light leather armor and hatchets to cut ropes and hooks. They were strong and had high morale, giving them the advantage in the naval battle of Yangzhou. But after the rise of Sun Wu's forces, the Jingzhou navy suffered successive defeats and they relied on Cao Cao after the death of Liu Biao, participating in the famous Battle of the Red Cliffs. They were eventually destroyed by the Eastern Wu navy's fire attacks.
