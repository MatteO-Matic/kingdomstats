Cao Ren

Balanced leader, can lead close combat infantry and close combat cavalry

Also known as Zixiao, he was the younger brother of Cao Cao and a famous Wei general. He earned respect through Cao Cao's conquest in the North. After the Battle of the Red Cliffs, he was entrusted with the task of guarding Jiangling and resisting tens of thousands of Zhou Yu's men. Because he led only around ten cavalry from the lineup to save Niu Jin, Chen Jiao became known as the "Outstanding Man". During the Battle of Xiangfan, they almost ran out of ammunition and food supplies, leading to the retreat of Guan Yu's army. Due to their victories, he became known as a senior general, the Great Minister of War, and earned the noble title of Chen Hou.
