He Qi

Military general, good at leading close combat infantry

Also known as Gongmiao, this Wu general was famous for his luxurious armory and for being a defender of honor. When submitting to the authority of the Sun, he helped Sun Ce stabilize the rebellions and set up Jian'an, Hanxing, and Nanping in Minyue. After Sun Quan came to the throne, He Qi, based on his victory against the Shanyue and pacification of many places, was honored as a Pingdong field officer, assistant general, and fenwu general. In the twelfth year of Jian'an, (215 AD), Sun Quan was defeated by Wei General Zhang Liao, after a collision with He Qi's army, but he was able to get away. He became a general in the end and was honored as Shanyinhou.
