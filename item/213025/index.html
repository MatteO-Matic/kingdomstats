Shu Skirmisher Cavalry

An army established by Liu Bei after defeating Shu, replacing the old army from Jingzhou.
They inherited some of the traditions of the Jingzhou army, wearing heavy leather armor with sabers.
They also had javelins that could shoot through leather. With the benefit of good equipment, they could complete harassment, assault, defense, and other tasks.
These cavalry troops were improved forces of the Jingzhou heavy cavalry, with more comprehensive and flexible tactics. They could also hold their own in battle.
