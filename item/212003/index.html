Nanzhong Infantry



Nanzhong was located in the south of Yizhou and was inhabited by a large number of Nanyi tribes who often rebelled against the Han Dynasty, whom they refused to give in to. After the founding of the Shu Han and the conquest controlling the chaotic Nanzhong area, the barbaric tribal warriors were incorporated into the army and became an important component of the Shu Han infantry. The Nanzhong Infantry were split into many groups and are equipped with cloth armor, using their own weapons and tools, and specializing in the use of poison. Their combat power was very reliable.
