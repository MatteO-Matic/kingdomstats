Jiefan Infantry

In 221 AD, Shu emperor Liu Bei attacked the Wu. Due to Sun Quan's lack of power, he sent his subordinate Hu Zong to all countries to recruit a thousand people. The "Solution Soldiers" were established in two branches, with Xu Xiang leading the left branch and Hu Zong leading the right. The "Solution Soldiers" were formed to resolve Sun Quan's worries about Liu Bei coming from the east. In the early period, the commanders of the split branch were Xu Xiang and Hu Zong. Later, due to the continuous worries of Wu ruler Sun Quan, the "Solution Soldiers" continued to expand and were temporarily commanded by senior generals in battle. Jiefan Infantry used spears and shields, wore heavy armor, and could effectively resist enemy infantry and cavalry, making them aces in the Eastern Wu's new army.
