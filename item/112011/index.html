Garrison Spears



The establishment of stationed soldiers was mainly due to the need for troops to be permanently stationed at the frontier to reduce the cost of transporting materials. Wei troops in Jingzhou and Hanzhong's defensive line often lacked stationed forces.
These forces were soldiers in battles, and farmers outside of them. Compared to the main force, stationed soldiers were often weak and could only handle defensive tasks, hindering and delaying enemies when invaded.
These stationed troops were made up of local people who were more dependable when defending their homeland, but their ability to attack could not be relied upon.
