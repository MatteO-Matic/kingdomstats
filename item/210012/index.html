Xuzhou Crossbowmen

After the Xuzhou army beat the Yellow Scarves, they developed slowly in peace time. Xuzhou crossbowmen were selected for the militia and equipped with a light crossbow and a single-handed knife. They went through basic training and could form a defense line against the enemy's arrows. In the battle between Tao Qian and Cao Cao, the Xuzhou army suffered heavy losses, with regions such as Pengcheng, Fuyang, Qulu, Juling, and Xiaqiu brutally defeated. Xuzhou soldiers relied on strong city defense and were barely able to hold onto the southern part of Xuzhou.
