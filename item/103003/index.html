Cai Mao

Commanding general, good at leading ranged infantry and close combat infantry



Also known as Degui, he was born of Xiangyang nobility and had a good relationship with Cao Cao. His sister was the second wife of general Liu Biao, who he helped in stabilizing Jingzhou. He was soon appointed as the general military advisor of Zhennan. When Liu Bei was in Jingzhou, Cai Mao, Kuai Yue, and others tried to harm him, but did not succeed. After the death of Liu Biao, Cai Mao and Zhang Yun wanted the young generation to take over and supported the second son of Liu Biao, Liu Cong, in taking over the position. After Cao Cao, Cai Mao became the Changshui field officer and became known as Hanyang Tinghou.
