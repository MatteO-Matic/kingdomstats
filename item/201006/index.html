Chen Dao

Military general, good at leading close combat infantry

Chen Dao, also known as Shuzhi, was a Shu Han General and governor of Yong'an from Runan, Yuzhou. Known to be loyal and brave, Chen Dao went to Liu Bei when serving as the highest ranking official of Yuzhou. This started a long battle with Liu Bei, with him leading the elite Shu Han forces, the Baier soldiers. During the Jianxing era, he became the "General Who Subdues the West". In the fourth year of the Jianxing era, he was stationed in Badong as Yongan governor, and died at his post.
