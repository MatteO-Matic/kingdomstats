Bingzhou Heavy Cavalry



Bingzhou's iron cavalry were originally the elite of the frontier fortress in the Eastern Han. With an outstanding performance in the battle against the Southern Xiongnu, they absorbed many Hu soldiers and became the main strength of Ding Yuan.
In the battle between Ding Yuan and Dong Zhuo, Lv Bu led them and defeated the Xiliang cavalry.
After Cao Cao defeated Lv Bu, he took over a portion of Lv Bu's elites, and some of the cavalry led by Zhang Liao may very well have been the cavalry.
In the following battles, they followed Zhang Liao and delivered a heavy blow to the Wu army in Xiaoyaojin, becoming a world-renowned powerhouse.
The Bingzhou Heavy Cavalry were skilled at using traditional frontal assault tactics. Their excellent mobility and strong penetration ability were unique in the Three Kingdoms era.
