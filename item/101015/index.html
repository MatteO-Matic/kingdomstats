Huangfu Song

Commanding general, good at leading ranged cavalry



Huangfu Song was a military general and strategist who lived in the Eastern Han dynasty. He was born in Liangzhou to a family of generals and he was famous for leading his troops to victory over the Yellow Turban forces in Changshe, Guangzong, Quyang etc. and playing a pivotal role in ultimately suppressing the Yellow Turban rebellion. Subsequently, he was also involved in the suppression of the North-West rebellion, the operation to wipe out Dong Zhuo's bloodline and he was the first important military figure during the fall of the Han dynasty.
