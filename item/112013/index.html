Yanzhou Halberdiers



Yanzhou is located in the Central Plains area, where a large number of civilians were conscripted into the army during the warring period.
After Cao Cao rose to power, his main strength was the Yanzhou army, a force formulated as the East Han Central Army's predecessor to Cao Wei's central troops.
Yanzhou Halberdiers wore heavy armor and military helmets, and the troop was made up of privates with battle experience.
They formed the defensive line behind the pikemen, jabbing forward and swinging at enemies in combat.
They were part of the elite of Yanzhou's army and were very active in the Warlord era.
