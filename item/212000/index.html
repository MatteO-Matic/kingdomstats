Xuzhou Militia



Militias have existed since ancient times and were active in military operations for all states and countries during the warring period of the Han Empire.
For the land of Tao Qian, Xuzhou once again came under attack by the Yellow Scarves after the Yellow Scarves Rebellion.
Tao Qian worked hard to cope after beating the Yellow Scarves and the destroyed Xuzhou began to resume development. At that time, Yanzhou and Qingzhou were at war, and massive amounts of refugees entered Xuzhou, expanding its militia.
Other than defending against the Yellow Scarves and bandit attacks, they also acted as government security forces, providing relief to victims and maintaining production.
Xuzhou's troops had complete equipment and enough food for field operations, and they also gained experience in facing the Yellow Scarves.
They became a group of reliable civilian armed forces.
