Village Shooters



Militias have existed since ancient times and were active in military operations for all states and countries during the warring period of the Han Empire.
The militia of the Jiangnan area was composed mainly of commoners who relied on powerful families to come together as soldiers.
The Village Shooters were made up of gentry members who often practiced archery to defend their manors. They could often be seen in their private residences and in government observation buildings.
If a battle broke out, the Village Shooters would gather together and accept commands from the gentry. Armed with a long bow, suitable for dealing with lightly equipped troops, they were formed of people from their own villages and rarely accepted outsiders.
Due to a lack of military training, they were easily defeated and taken over by Sun Ce during the conquests in Jiangdong.
