Shu Crossbow Riders

An army established by Liu Bei after defeating Shu, replacing the old army from Jingzhou.
They inherited some of the traditions of the Jingzhou army. Shu crossbow riders moved quickly and their shooting was accurate and deadly.
They were used to replace the insufficient numbers of the Jingzhou Light Cavalry. These cavalrymen often used light crossbows and formed a ring around the enemy to shoot at them on the battlefield.
Fast loading and accurate attacks were their specialty, but a lack of protection and weak melee skills meant they had to keep their distance from heavily armored enemy troops.
