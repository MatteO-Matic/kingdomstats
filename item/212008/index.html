Shu Royal Strike Troop



After the establishment of the Shu Han, much of the Bashu Han army was taken over. These soldiers were selected and formed a new army to replace the reduced Jingzhou army. The Shu forces were mainly composed of Liu Zhang's selection of thirty thousand elite refugees. They were more loyal to outside rulers than the Shu people and the elite of the eastern state soldiers. There were also old department members of the Jingzhou army among them, who fought in the years of Liu Bei. These forces were well-equipped and in charge of guarding the imperial guards and the palace. In the battle during Liu Bei's expedition to Eastern Wu, they protected him, allowing him to retreat safely. Although the guards all came from the military elite, they faced a serious decline in combat proficiency due to the fact that they went for long periods without fighting.
