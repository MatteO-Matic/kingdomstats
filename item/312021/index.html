Wu Garrison Battalion



Militias have existed since ancient times and were active in military operations for all states and countries during the warring period of the Han Empire. Wu garrison battalion was one of militia. They usually cultivated to store grain and entered battlefield as replacement in wartime. Due to poor equipment and lack of training, they only acted as assistant role.
