Niu Jin

Commanding general, good at leading close combat cavalry



Cao Wei General. In his early years he was once under Cao Ren. In the third year of the Qinglong era (235 AD), Shu General Ma Dai invaded. The army, led by Niu Jin, achieved complete victory. In the second year of the Jingchu era (238 AD), allies such as Hu Zun followed Si Mayi in the rebellion against Gong Sunyuan and pacified Liaodong. In the end, he was made a general.
